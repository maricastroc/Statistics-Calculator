Math Operations
Arithmetic Average

The arithmetic mean simply involves taking the sum of a group of numbers, then dividing that sum by the count of the numbers used in the series.

Median

Median is the point above and below which half (50%) the observed data falls, and so represents the midpoint of the data.

Mode

The mode is the value that appears most frequently in a data set. A set of data may have one mode, more than one mode, or no mode at all.

Weighted Average

Weighted average takes into account the varying degrees of importance of the numbers in a data set - each number in the data set is multiplied by a predetermined weight.
